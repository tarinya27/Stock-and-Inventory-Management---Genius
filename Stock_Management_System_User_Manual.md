# Stock Management System
## User Manual for Store Manager

---

---

## 2. Create Category

1. From the **Left Navigation Panel**, click **Categories**.
2. Click **Create Category**.
3. Enter the category name and save.

---

## 3. Products Section

- **Search and filter products** by category using the search and filter options.
- **Add bulk products** using the **Create / Stock In Product** button.
- **Edit purchase and selling prices** using the Edit (pencil) icon next to each product.

---

## 4. Stock In (Receiving Products from Outside)

1. Go to **Stock In** from the navigation.
2. Add products received from outside suppliers.
3. Enter product details, quantity, and other required information.
4. Save the stock-in record.

---

## 5. Scan Barcode Section

- Search for product availability by **product name** or **barcode**.
- Use the scan or search field to find products quickly.

---

## 6. Stock Out Process

1. Go to **Scan Barcode**.
2. Search for the product by name or barcode.
3. Click **Stock Out** on the product.
4. Fill in the required details (quantity, reason, etc.).
5. Click **Save**.

---

## 7. Stock Sheet

1. Go to **Stock Sheet** from the navigation.
2. View the current stock levels.
3. Download the stock sheet as needed.

---

## 8. Reports Section

1. Go to **Reports** from the navigation.
2. View available reports.
3. Use filters and options to generate the reports you need.

---

## 9. Low Stock Alerts

1. Click the **Bell Icon** on the Dashboard.
2. Go to the **Low Stock / Alert** section.
3. View the list of low stock items.
4. Download the sheet if needed.
